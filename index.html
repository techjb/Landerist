Landerist: Real decentralization of Real Estate

Mission:
  • Decentralize the real estate advertisement market.
Approach:
  • Read the millions of real estate listings that real estate agencies post on their websites.
  • Use artificial intelligence to extract relevant data from each listing and publish the entire dataset openly and indefinitely.
  • Keep the entire dataset constantly updated.
Team:

Traction:
