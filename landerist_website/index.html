The open data layer for real estate listings.

Daily updated, AI-normalized property listings from thousands of agency websites.

Approach

  1. Collect listings from the source.

    We process property listings directly from thousands of real estate agency websites, beyond centralized portals.

  2. Normalize them with AI.

    Each listing is cleaned, structured and converted into a consistent format for search, analysis and integration.

  3. Keep the data fresh.

    The dataset is updated daily to capture new listings, price changes, removals and market activity.
